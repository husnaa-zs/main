Why Dark Mode?

  • History

    The Origin of Dark Mode

    Dark mode was originally used in early computer systems due to the limitations of CRT monitors. It has since evolved into a feature that reduces eye strain and improves battery life.

  • Health

    Eye Comfort & Health Benefits

    Dark mode reduces the amount of blue light emitted by screens, which helps prevent eye fatigue and supports better sleep when using devices at night.

  • Technology

    Energy Efficiency

    On OLED and AMOLED screens, dark mode saves power by turning off pixels, extending battery life on smartphones and tablets.

  • Design

    Modern Aesthetic Appeal

    Many users find dark mode visually appealing and elegant. It allows colorful elements and images to pop more clearly against a dark background.
